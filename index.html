Know About Us

Why CAEA can improve
your English conversation skills so much

Reason 1: CAEA's pride :
"Complete one-on-one service"

CAEA doesn't have group lessons where you worry about what others think or can't listen to things you don't understand. Because the lessons are completely one-on-one, you can feel free to ask anything you don't understand as many times as you like, and you can talk as much as you like without fear of making mistakes.

Reason 2: A study program that
“reliably” improves English skills for people of all ages

CAEA has set up a unique learning program that specializes in "learning to speak English." We have created a curriculum specifically focused on acquiring listening and speaking skills, which are weaknesses of Japanese people .

Reason 3 : We have a spacious private room where you can hear the pronunciation clearly.

For one-on-one lessons, we provide a large private room for each student so you won't feel cramped even if you stay for a long time . The ``yellow egg foam'' installed on the ceiling of the classroom is ``to prevent echoes.'' Since you can hear the pronunciation clearly, you can concentrate on studying.

Flow of admission

8 steps to admission

Step 1
Information confirmation/consultation

If you are interested in studying abroad in CAEA, please take a look at this website first. If you have any questions or concerns, please also check the Frequently Asked Questions . If you have any questions, please contact us . Our Japanese staff will answer your questions.

Step 2
Application for admission

Once you have decided to enroll at Kaea, please apply from this "Application" page. In principle, our school has check-in on Sunday and check-out on Saturday , but we also accept enrollment on other days of the week. Also, please be sure to click and read the application terms and school regulations . Once your application is complete, Kaea will send you an email with instructions regarding the procedure. Hotel check-out time is 12:00 pm and check-in time is after 2:00 pm, so check-in before that time is normally counted as check-in on the previous day, but only international students can check-in before 12:00 noon. Check-in is considered the day before, and check-in after 12:00 is considered same-day check-in. If you already have a passport , please check the expiration date. ( Please check the Philippine Consulate website for the remaining validity period ) If you do not have a passport , the process will take 1 to 2 weeks.

Step 3
Payment of study abroad fees

Once you have submitted your study abroad application form, Kaea will contact you via email regarding further procedures. Once you receive the email notification from Kaea, we will send you the 30,000 yen application fee or the entire study abroad fee (admission fee + study abroad fee + 2,500 yen remittance fee) per applicant within 3 banking days from the next day. Please transfer. Please note that international students are responsible for the transfer fee. At Kaair, we will never allow instructors to appear in front of our students if they have not completed our prescribed training period. Since it is completely one-on-one instruction, you can assume that the number of international students equals the number of instructors, but we always train an additional number of instructors so that we can accept all the international students who apply. However, it is not possible to develop high-quality instructors with good teaching skills overnight. If we do not have enough instructors to satisfy international students, we will not easily accept those who wish to study abroad, and we may have no choice but to refuse admission. In order to ensure acceptance of all international students, we ask that you apply as early as possible. If there are less than 30 days left until enrollment at the time of application and you are accepted to our school, you will be required to transfer the full amount as soon as possible , regardless of your bank's 3 business day rule . A transfer certificate issued by a financial institution will be used in place of the receipt. Once you have made the transfer, we will confirm the payment, so please be sure to let us know by email.

Step 4
Arranging flight tickets

Please purchase your own flight ticket. Please purchase the most suitable air ticket that matches the check-in and check-out dates you entered when applying for study abroad.

When leaving Japan and entering the Philippines , be sure to purchase a return ticket. If you know your return date, you can purchase a ticket even months later. If you have a ticket for your return date, you can leave the country without any problems even if your return date is 6 months later. If you are unsure of your return date, you will need to present a ticket to a third country other than the Philippines.A "throwaway ticket" that is set for a date and time within 30 days from the date of entry.For example, a ticket to Kota Kinabalu in Malaysia costs less than 10,000 yen. If you cancel after entering the Philippines, the amount may be refunded depending on the agency. On the way back, you will need to buy a one-way ticket. Or please purchase an "open ticket" .

Step 5
Arranging overseas travel accident insurance

Kaea recommends international students to take out overseas travel accident insurance when traveling. It is advantageous to make a reservation online. If you use credit card supplementary insurance (which comes free with your card), please check with your credit card company to make sure that the coverage is acceptable to you. If you want to take advantage of the free insurance provided by your credit card, please visit the Japanese Help Desk and choose a card that supports cashless transactions. If Tokio Marine & Nichido becomes your partner, we cannot accept cashless payments , so you will have to pay the full amount yourself and then be billed after you return to your country.

Step 6
Preparation/What to bring

Once you have made your flight ticket arrangements, please use the following page to complete the "Advance Level Check Test", "Application for Airport Pickup Request", and "Application for SSP Acquisition Items". Please complete this at least two weeks before your enrollment date.

Step 7
Departure

Please arrive at the airport 2 hours before departure time. In the event of an emergency, such as a change in flight time, please send an email.

Step 8
Arrival in Cebu [Pick-up available 24 hours]

The staff of Hotel Asia, where you will be staying, will be available 24 hours a day to pick you up at Cebu Mactan International Airport. We will send pick-up instructions to international students in advance.
