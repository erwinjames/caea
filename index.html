Know About Us

Why CAEA can improve
your English conversation skills so much

Reason 1: CAEA's pride :
"Complete one-on-one service"

CAEA doesn't have group lessons where you worry about what others think or can't listen to things you don't understand. Because the lessons are completely one-on-one, you can feel free to ask anything you don't understand as many times as you like, and you can talk as much as you like without fear of making mistakes.

Reason 2: A study program that
“reliably” improves English skills for people of all ages

CAEA has set up a unique learning program that specializes in "learning to speak English." We have created a curriculum specifically focused on acquiring listening and speaking skills, which are weaknesses of Japanese people .

Reason 3 : We have a spacious private room where you can hear the pronunciation clearly.

For one-on-one lessons, we provide a large private room for each student so you won't feel cramped even if you stay for a long time . The ``yellow egg foam'' installed on the ceiling of the classroom is ``to prevent echoes.'' Since you can hear the pronunciation clearly, you can concentrate on studying.

Flow of admission

8 steps to admission

Step 1
Information confirmation/consultation

Interested in studying abroad with CAEA? Visit / Click this link www.caea-ph.com and check the FAQs for more information. For further inquiries, contact our Japanese staff directly.

Step 2
Application for admission

To enroll at Kaea, apply through the "Application" page and read the application terms and school regulations. Check-in is on Sunday and check-out on Saturday, but other days are accepted. After applying, Kaea will email you instructions. Hotel check-out is at 12:00 pm and check-in is after 2:00 pm, with early check-in options for international students. Ensure your passport is valid, and if you don't have one, it takes 1-2 weeks to process.

Step 3
Payment of study abroad fees

After submitting your application, Kaea will email you the next steps. Transfer the 30,000 yen application fee or the full study abroad fee within 3 banking days and confirm payment by email. Apply early to ensure availability, as instructor training limits intake.

Step 4
Arranging flight tickets

Purchase your own flight ticket matching your study abroad check-in and check-out dates. When leaving Japan and entering the Philippines, buy a return ticket or a ticket to a third country within 30 days. Consider an "open ticket" for flexibility.

Step 5
Arranging overseas travel accident insurance

For international travel, Kaea recommends obtaining overseas travel accident insurance, preferably through online reservation. Verify coverage with your credit card company if using supplementary insurance. Note that cashless payments may not be accepted if Tokio Marine & Nichido is the insurance provider.

Step 6
Preparation/What to bring

Once you have made your flight ticket arrangements, please use the following page to complete the "Advance Level Check Test", "Application for Airport Pickup Request", and "Application for SSP Acquisition Items". Please complete this at least two weeks before your enrollment date.

Step 7
Departure

Please arrive at the airport 2 hours before departure time. In the event of an emergency, such as a change in flight time, please send an email.

Step 8
Arrival in Cebu [Pick-up available 24 hours]

The staff of Hotel Asia, where you will be staying, will be available 24 hours a day to pick you up at Cebu Mactan International Airport. We will send pick-up instructions to international students in advance.
