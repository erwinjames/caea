Know About Us

Why CAEA can improve
your English conversation skills so much

Reason 1: CAEA's pride :
"Complete one-on-one service"

CAEA doesn't have group lessons where you worry about what others think or can't listen to things you don't understand. Because the lessons are completely one-on-one, you can feel free to ask anything you don't understand as many times as you like, and you can talk as much as you like without fear of making mistakes.

Reason 2: A study program that
“reliably” improves English skills for people of all ages

CAEA has set up a unique learning program that specializes in "learning to speak English." We have created a curriculum specifically focused on acquiring listening and speaking skills, which are weaknesses of Japanese people .

Reason 3 : We have a spacious private room where you can hear the pronunciation clearly.

For one-on-one lessons, we provide a large private room for each student so you won't feel cramped even if you stay for a long time . The ``yellow egg foam'' installed on the ceiling of the classroom is ``to prevent echoes.'' Since you can hear the pronunciation clearly, you can concentrate on studying.

More About Us

A brief introduction to the one-on-one lessons

The people taking this class are in their 60s who enrolled as ``complete beginners''

At CAEA, we offer Japanese hotels where Japanese people can learn English to their heart's content in a safe environment.

Living in an unfamiliar environment is full of anxiety. But don't worry.
CAEA's ``hotel'', ``English school'', and ``Japanese restaurant'' are all operated by the same owner/manager.
